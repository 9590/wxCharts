Detailed Description

This class is used to draw one of the segments of a circular chart e.g. a pie chart or a doughnut chart.

Constructs a wxChartArc element. The arc is defined by the coordinates of its centre (x, y), its inner radius and its outer radius. The arc goes from the starting angle startAngle to endAngle clockwise. The angles are measured in radians but, contrary to the usual mathematical convention, are always clockwise from the horizontal axis.

Parameters
xThe X coordinate of the center of the arc.
yThe Y coordinate of the center of the arc.
startAngleThe angle (in radians) at which the arc starts.
endAngleThe angle (in radians) at which the arc ends.
outerRadiusThe outer radius of the arc.
innerRadiusThe inner radius of the arc. This can be 0.
tooltipThe tooltip.
optionsThe settings to be used for the arc.
